Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, better known as BMW, was founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany. Originally an aircraft engine manufacturer, BMW shifted its focus to automobiles in the 1920s and quickly became a global symbol of precision engineering and luxury performance. The company’s dedication to innovation and excellence laid the foundation for what would later become one of its most iconic divisions: BMW M. Created in 1972 as BMW Motorsport GmbH, this team was formed to support BMW's racing ambitions and push the boundaries of performance technology.

The BMW M Series represents the high-performance branch of BMW, known for producing some of the most thrilling and advanced vehicles on the road. From the legendary M3 to the track-ready M4 and the powerful M5, each model blends motorsport heritage with everyday drivability. BMW M cars are engineered by a dedicated team that continuously innovates in speed, handling, and design. Their work isn't just about building cars—it's about crafting an experience that reflects BMW's commitment to "The Ultimate Driving Machine."
